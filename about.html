About Cap Squeeze

We got tired of boring water. So we built something better.

Every day, millions of Indians drink plain water — not because they love it, but because the alternatives are loaded with sugar, artificial colors, and chemicals they cannot pronounce. We wanted to change that.

Cap Squeeze was founded with one simple idea: what if you could turn any regular water bottle into a delicious, zero-sugar flavoured drink — just by swapping the cap? That's exactly what we built. India's first flavoured bottle cap. No fancy equipment. No subscription boxes. No expensive imports. Just a cap, a natural flavour pod, and better hydration — starting at ₹149.

Our flavour pods use only premium natural extracts, organic Stevia leaf, and functional nutrients like electrolytes and Vitamin C. Everything is FSSAI certified, manufactured in India, and designed to work with the water bottles you already own.

Our Mission

To make healthy hydration the most convenient, enjoyable, and affordable choice for every Indian. Whether you're at the gym, in class, at your desk, or on a morning run — Cap Squeeze makes water worth drinking.

Made in India 🇮🇳

Proudly built and manufactured in India. We support local manufacturing, use locally sourced ingredients wherever possible, and ship to every pin code across the country. Free shipping above ₹499. COD available.
