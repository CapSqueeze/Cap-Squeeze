The Magic Behind Cap Squeeze

Three steps. Infinite flavor.

Cap Squeeze is the simplest way to make your water taste amazing. Twist, squeeze, and enjoy.

The Simplest Upgrade to Water

No powders to mix, no messy drops. Just pure, natural flavor infused instantly as you drink.

1

Choose Your Flavor

Select from our premium lineup of 12+ natural flavour pods. Whether you need morning energy (Matcha Focus) or post-workout refreshment (Lime Replenish).

2

Twist On The Cap

Unscrew the original cap from any standard 28mm water bottle (like Bisleri, Kinley, or your reusable metal bottle) and twist Cap Squeeze right on.

3

Squeeze & Enjoy

Simply squeeze the bottle and drink. Water passes through our patented internal flavor core, delivering perfectly calibrated flavor with every sip.

Anatomy of the Cap

We spent 18 months engineering the perfect fluid dynamics system to ensure you get consistent flavor from the first sip to the last drop.

  • 01

    Smart-Flow Nozzle

    A zero-spill silicone valve that only releases liquid when pressure is applied, preventing accidental leaks in your gym bag.

  • 02

    Flavor Infusion Core

    Contains 100% natural fruit extracts and Stevia. Specially designed micro-channels mix the exact right amount of flavor as water flows through.

  • 03

    Universal Threading (28mm)

    Precision-engineered to fit snugly onto 95% of standard packaged water bottles available in India, providing an airtight seal.
