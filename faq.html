Product & Usage

Cap Squeeze is India's first flavoured bottle cap. It's a specially designed cap with a built-in flavour pod that screws onto any standard water bottle. When you drink, water passes through the pod and gets naturally flavoured — zero sugar, zero mixing, instant taste.
One Cap Squeeze pod is designed to perfectly flavor up to 3 liters (approximately 3-4 standard bottles) of water. The flavor intensity will naturally taper off as you reach the 3-liter mark.
Yes. Our caps have a shelf life of 9 months when sealed in their original airtight foil packaging. Once opened and screwed onto a bottle, we recommend consuming it within 3-4 days for the freshest flavor.

Compatibility

Cap Squeeze features a universal 28mm PCO 1881 thread. This means it fits 95% of standard packaged water bottles (like Bisleri, Kinley, Aquafina) as well as standard soft drink bottles (Coca-Cola, Pepsi) in India.
Absolutely! Using Cap Squeeze with sparkling or carbonated water creates a delicious, zero-sugar healthy soda alternative. Just be careful when opening to release the pressure slowly.
Yes, if your reusable metal or plastic bottle has a standard 28mm neck (often found on basic insulated bottles). It will not fit wide-mouth bottles (like Nalgene or HydroFlask) without an adapter.
