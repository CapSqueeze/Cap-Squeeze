India's First Flavoured Bottle Cap

Cap Squeeze is India's first flavoured bottle cap hydration brand. Our zero-sugar natural flavour caps screw onto any standard 28mm water bottle — Bisleri, Kinley, Aquafina, and most common Indian water bottles — and instantly transform plain water into a delicious, healthy flavoured drink. No mixing. No mess. Just twist, sip, and enjoy.

With 12+ all-natural flavors including Mango Fizz, Lemon Zest, Strawberry Burst, and Matcha Focus, Cap Squeeze is the healthy alternative to sugary drinks and artificially flavoured beverages. Every pod is sweetened with organic Stevia, FSSAI certified, made in India, and contains zero sugar, zero artificial colors, and just 3 calories per serving.

Loved by gym-goers, students, working professionals, and health-conscious parents across India. Starting at just ₹149 per cap. Free shipping on orders above ₹499. COD available. Delivering across India in 3-5 business days.
